### What is the Word?

**Have**

### Tamil Meaning:

வேண்டும்

### Explanation about it:

* Meaning for this is `vaithiruthal`
	* I have a phone
* Third person singular point of view
	* He has phone

```
I, we, you, they  ==> Have
He, she it ==> Has
```

* In question `vachurikingala?`
	* Do you have a phone?
