### What is the Word?

**Be** -- Will you **be** my friend?

### Tamil Meaning:

* Be - இரு

### What is that mean?

* `be` is a verb.
	* Verb going to happen.

### Why we are using it?

#### In simple present tense:

* Be safe
* Be ready
* Be Careful
* Exam is to **be** conducted
