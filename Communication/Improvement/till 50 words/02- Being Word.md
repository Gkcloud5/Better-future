### What is the Word?

**Being**

### Tamil Meaning:

* இருப்பது

### Explanation:

* Generally it comes with continuation

##### Present continues
###### Passive:
* Your payment is being processed.
* The story is being written

##### Past continues:
* Your payment was being processed
* The story was
