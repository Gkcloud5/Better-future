### What it is:

* TLD is the first stop after the root zone.
* Is everything following the final dot of domain name.
	* Example: `google.com` here `.com` is the TLD.
		* .org
		* .com
		* .uk
		* .edu
* `.com` is intended for commercial businesses
* `.gov` is for government
